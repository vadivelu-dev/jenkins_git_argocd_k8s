Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about our parking services? Check out our most frequently asked questions below.

1. How do I book a parking spot?

You can book a parking spot through our website or mobile app. Simply search for parking near your destination, select your preferred spot, choose your duration, and complete the payment.

2. Can I cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can cancel your reservation up to 1 hour before your scheduled time for a full refund. Cancellations within 1 hour are subject to a small processing fee.

3. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and other digital wallet options. All transactions are secure and encrypted.

4. Is my car safe in your parking facilities?

Absolutely! All our parking locations are secured with CCTV monitoring, proper lighting, and regular security patrols. We also offer insurance options for additional peace of mind.

5. Do you offer monthly parking subscriptions?

Yes! We offer monthly parking subscriptions at discounted rates. This is perfect for daily commuters and regular parkers. Contact us for more details on available locations and pricing.
